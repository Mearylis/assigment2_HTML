What are Souls-like games?

Souls-like is an action-RPG subgenre inspired by FromSoftware's Dark Souls series. These games are known for their high difficulty, atmospheric world design, and deep combat system.

Key features of the genre:

  • High difficulty and punishment for mistakes
  • Currency loss on death with retrieval mechanics
  • Interconnected game world
  • Minimalist storytelling through environment
  • Tactical combat system
